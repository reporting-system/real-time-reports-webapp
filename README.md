# AmbiTrack

Collaborative Environmental Monitoring Web Application

AmbiTrack is a web-based application designed to support environmental monitoring through the collection, visualization, and management of georeferenced environmental reports. It enables real-time awareness and informed decision-making through interactive mapping and alert mechanisms.

---

## Overview

AmbiTrack centralizes environmental incident reports submitted by users and organizations, presenting them on an interactive map with filtering and tracking capabilities. The platform is designed to be lightweight, accessible, and usable across different devices and connectivity conditions.

---

## Purpose

The main purpose of AmbiTrack is to provide a digital tool that facilitates:

- Reporting environmental incidents in a structured and georeferenced manner.
- Visualizing environmental data through interactive maps.
- Supporting timely response through early alert mechanisms.
- Organizing environmental information for monitoring and analysis.

---

## Target Users

- Individuals interested in reporting and tracking environmental issues.
- Local communities and organizations involved in environmental protection.
- Institutions and teams responsible for environmental monitoring and response.

---

## Key Features

- User registration and role-based access management.
- Submission of georeferenced environmental reports with descriptions and media.
- Interactive map visualization with filters and layers.
- Early warning alerts for critical or recurring incidents.
- Document and data management tools.
- Administrative dashboard with metrics and activity tracking.
- Responsive interface for desktop and mobile devices.

---

## Scope

- Web-based application accessible through modern browsers.
- Designed for scalability and future feature expansion.
- No dependency on external hardware or sensors in the current version.

---

## Technical Approach

- Modular architecture to support maintainability and scalability.
- Optimized performance for low-bandwidth environments.
- Focus on usability and clarity for different user profiles.
